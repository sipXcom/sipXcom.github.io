Welcome to the sipXcom Project.

sipXcom has its roots in SIPfoundry’s sipX and sipXecs projects. eZuce Inc. is the primary sponsor of sipXcom and from 2010 until February 2015 was the primary sponsor and contributor to sipXecs.

Due to changes at SIPfoundry we’ve decided to fork sipXecs and continue development of the code in the new sipXcom project.

Please bear with us as we get the new web site up and running and some of our infrastructure swapped over!

We’re currently publishing new builds at: http://download.sipxcom.org

What is sipXcom?

sipXcom is a modular and scalable communications solution for enterprises of all sizes. It provides a highly available SIP routing core integrated with a suite of communications services all managed through a unified web based management application. sipXcom provides traditional PBX telephony services integrated with instant messaging, and allows the use of advanced communications tools like video calling
